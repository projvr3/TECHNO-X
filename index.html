About

TECHNO-X is a dynamic and innovative association operating under the Computer Science and Engineering branch of the engineering college. It serves as a hub for tech enthusiasts, fostering a culture of learning, creativity, and collaboration. The association organizes a variety of events, such as technical workshops, coding competitions, seminars, and cultural activities, aiming to enhance both technical expertise and soft skills among its members. TECHNO-X is dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, empowering students to excel in academics, technology, and beyond.

To accomplish recognition in computer science and engineering by producing technical professionals with ethical values .

Other Events

FRESHERS DAY

The Freshers Day is an event conducted every year in January, to welcome new students into the department with a variety of fun and creative events that unleashes the knack of the students in various aspects. This event seeks to kick-start the relationship between junior and seniors, where the seniors provide a heartwarming welcome to the juniors and aspire to start working and learning with them. Piloting the interactive environment of seniors and juniors, it paves way for the juniors to explore themselves and unwrap their potential.

INDUSTRY VISIT

An industrial visit to the U R Rao Satellite Centre (URSC), located in Bengaluru, provides a unique learning experience for students to explore the advanced field of space technology. URSC, a key facility of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), is responsible for the design and development of satellites for various applications like communication, navigation, and earth observation. During the visit, students can witness the assembly, integration, and testing of satellites and gain insights into cutting-edge technologies used in space missions. It offers a rare opportunity to interact with scientists and engineers, enhancing their understanding of satellite systems and inspiring them towards careers in science and technology.
